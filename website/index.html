Questions

How does the deepfake detection work?

Verilens runs real forensic AI models — an image forensics model, a multimodal video model, and a text classifier — plus local C2PA/SynthID provenance checks. It keeps each result separate so you always know what was flagged and why.

Is my data sent anywhere?

Detection runs against the model backends only when you explicitly click "Check." Nothing is stored or sold. Provenance checks happen locally in your browser.

What happens when I click Purchase?

Clicking Purchase signals your installed Verilens extension to unlock Pro on your device.

Can I cancel?

Pro is a simple toggle in this build — you can switch back to Free anytime from the extension's settings.
