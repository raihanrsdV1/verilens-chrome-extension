👋 What does Verilens do?

Verilens adds small AI-powered checks directly into your social feed (X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook). It never posts or changes anything — it only analyzes what you choose to check and shows you the result.

🔍 "Check media" button

Appears under posts with an image or video. Click it to ask an AI model "is this real or AI-generated?" The result shows as a colored badge:

Likely real — looks like genuine, unedited media.
Uncertain — the model isn't confident either way.
Likely AI / fake — strong signs of AI generation or manipulation.

Image checks are free. Video (and audio) checks need heavier multimodal models and are part of Premium.

✅ Confirmed AI badges (C2PA / SynthID)

Some images carry an invisible, cryptographically signed "AI-made" label from the tool that created them (e.g. C2PA Content Credentials or Google SynthID). When Verilens finds one, it shows "Confirmed AI" immediately — this is stronger evidence than a model's guess, since it's a verified signature, not an estimate.

📰 "Fact-check" button

Appears under posts with text. Click it to have an AI agent look up the claims in the post against trusted sources and show you whether each claim looks true, false, or unverifiable — with links to what it found. This is a Premium feature since it does live research.
